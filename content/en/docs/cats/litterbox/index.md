#### changing the bag

1. pull the bottom drawer out
2. take bag out
3. put a new bag in
4. top off with a little bit of litter
   1. there is a max-fill line on the liner
5. press the reset button
6. throw the bag away

   ![Image](reset.png 'reset button')
